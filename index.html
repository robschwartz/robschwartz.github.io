Amozin

Amozin

I built this site as part of a 5-day project at DevBootcamp. The original idea was to create a replica of amazon that sold fictional items. The main point of the project was to practice skills I have learnt. I used ROR, Javascript, RESTful development, HTML5, CSS3, and Owl Carousel. For testing we used rspec, capybara, Coverall, and Codeship for continuous deployment. The design was not the point of the project. The main point was practicing TDD and the technologies we've learnt.

Browsin'

Browsin'

I worked on this site for my 8 day final project at DBC. The app is designed for an audience that has multiple premium media services. For now, the sites we include are HBO Go, Hulu+, and Netflix. The app allows you to search through all of the content that is available to you based on your subscriptions to make your search for something to watch quick and painless. The goal of our app is to have you in and out so you can search less, and watch more.

The site was built with React.JS, OO JS, Ruby, Rails, AJAX, Jquery, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3.
